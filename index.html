CAMS

  • Culture
  • Automation
  • Measurement
  • Sharing

{% aside %} culture: trust, collaboration, continuous improvemtn automation: ability to provision and deploy without manual steps measurement: knowing what the business goals are and whether your improvemts are adding business value sharing: using the same source control, the same automation, the same measuring systems and the same targets {% aside %}
